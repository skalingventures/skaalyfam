Mission 1 — Outdoor Strike
Morning at Wash Park + Duck Trail
1
When Sat 9:30–11:30 AM
Where Washington Park
Drive 8 min
Cost $0–12
The Plan

Hit Wash Park by 9:30 before the crowds arrive. Odie leads the duck trail loop at full sprint — let him set the pace. Mazzy rides in the carrier, gets the fresh air and motion she loves. After the loop, grab coffees from Wash Perk and let Odie hit the playground for 20 minutes before heading home ahead of Mazzy's first nap.

Why This Weekend

Saturday is the weather gem of the two days — 62°F and barely any wind. This is the window before spring weekend crowds fully arrive. The duck trail gives Odie a mission with no setup required and Mazzy a smooth ride in the carrier.

Logistics
  • Carrier for Mazzy — trail isn't stroller-friendly
  • Snacks for Odie in the car (he'll ask the second you leave)
  • Layer up — 41°F at 9:30, warms fast by 10:30
  • Leave by 11:15 to beat Mazzy's nap window
☁ Weather Gate

If Saturday drops below 45°F or rain hits — swap to Denver Museum of Nature & Science. Same morning window, Odie loves the dinosaurs, everyone stays warm.

Mission 2 — In-Town Op
Meow Wolf + Early Dinner on Santa Fe
2
When Sat 3:30–5:30 PM
Where Santa Fe Arts District
Drive 12 min
Cost ~$45 ★ splurge
The Plan

Afternoon window after Mazzy's second nap. Grab an early dinner at Punch Bowl Social (loud, kid-tolerant, Odie can handle it), then walk through Meow Wolf Denver. Odie is exactly the right age for the sensory overload — he'll lose his mind in the best way. Mazzy in the carrier, you both get to actually experience something weird and memorable.

Why This Weekend

The 3:30 PM Saturday slot is a sweet spot — Odie past post-nap grogginess, Mazzy freshly fed, and Meow Wolf's afternoon crowd is lighter than evenings. With Sunday looking windier, Saturday afternoon is the move for something bigger.

Logistics
  • Buy Meow Wolf tickets online in advance — they sell out weekends
  • Carrier for Mazzy — stroller is cumbersome inside
  • Tell Odie 45 minutes before you walk in, or he'll never leave
  • Parking: street on Kalamath usually open Saturday afternoon
☁ Weather Gate

Fully indoors — weather-proof. If Sunday looks worse than forecast, you can flip Missions 1 and 2 across the days without losing anything.
